## 3. Permitted Uses — No Permission Required

You may use the Vibrante-Node name and logo without requesting permission in the following contexts:

### 3.1 Referential and Factual Use

- Accurately stating that your product **integrates with**, **is compatible with**, or **is a plugin for** Vibrante-Node.
  - Correct: *"VFX-Bridge — a plugin for Vibrante-Node"*
  - Correct: *"Tested on Vibrante-Node v2.0.0"*

- Writing articles, tutorials, reviews, or documentation about Vibrante-Node.

- Discussing Vibrante-Node in academic, educational, or conference contexts.

### 3.2 Compatible Plugin Naming

When naming a third-party plugin or node pack, you may use the form:

> **[YourName] for Vibrante-Node**

You must not use:

> **Vibrante-Node [Descriptor]** — this implies official origin

### 3.3 Community Use

Open-source forks, community repositories, and derivative projects may reference Vibrante-Node to describe their origin, provided the name clearly distinguishes the fork from the official project.

Acceptable fork naming patterns:
- `[ForkName] (based on Vibrante-Node)`
- `[ForkName] — fork of Vibrante-Node`

## 4. Prohibited Uses

The following uses are **not permitted** without explicit written permission from the trademark holder:

### 4.1 Impersonation and False Association

- Naming a product or service **Vibrante-Node** or anything confusingly similar that could imply it is the official project.
- Using the logo or name in a manner that implies official endorsement, partnership, or affiliation when none exists.
- Registering domain names containing **vibrante-node** or **vibrantenode** for commercial purposes without written permission.

### 4.2 Deceptive or Disparaging Use

- Using the name or logo in a context that is false, misleading, or disparaging to the project or its author.
- Implying that a commercial product is "official" Vibrante-Node software without a formal licensing agreement.

### 4.3 Logo Modification

- Altering, distorting, recolouring, or incorporating the Vibrante-Node logo into another logo without written permission.
- Removing copyright notices or attribution strings from the logo files.

### 4.4 Merchandise and Commercial Branding
